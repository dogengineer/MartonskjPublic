Nascondi i capezzoloni

Creavduris is a project aimed at raising awareness on the issues of pain and rebirth. The series “loving my body”, addresses the issue of insecurities and fragility related to one’s own body, fostered by a society that regularly tells us how we are supposed to be. At the same time, the series reflects on the possibility of rebirth as a proud acceptance of one’s own body.
